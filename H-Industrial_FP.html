Industrial / Goth 
Futurepop 
AKA: Synthpop, Pop EBM //  Fines 90´s

Trance came from German EBM. The baseline of Trance's multifarious origins has always been EBM producers messing around with arpeggiators.

Futurepop is simply Trance finding its way back to its EBM roots.Some don't like this music. In fact, in the Industrial-Goth scene it's not even called Futurepop. They pejoratively call it Synthpop (and some of them disown in completely).
